About SHIFት

We're building the bridge between African universities and the world of work.

Our Mission

Empowering African students with real-world experience

SHIFT connects university students across Africa with meaningful work opportunities before they graduate, ensuring they enter the job market with practical skills and experience that make them valuable contributors from day one.

Our Vision

A future where every African graduate is workforce-ready

We envision a future where the gap between education and employment in Africa is bridged, where students graduate not just with theoretical knowledge but with practical skills, professional networks, and the confidence to succeed in their careers.

Our Story

How SHIFT came to be

A Message from Our CEO

"SHIFT started with a moment I’ll never forget. I walked into a campus restroom and saw a paper used as a pad. That single image stuck with me—it said everything about how much our students are struggling just to survive. And I couldn’t unsee it. It made me ask: what if we built a system where students don’t just survive—but thrive? That was the beginning. SHIFT started as a simple but powerful idea: to create a platform where students could use their skills to earn income, gain real experience, and build their future—starting with a digital part-time job system. We realized that students aren’t short on talent. They’re short on opportunity, connection, and support. So we started building something that could shift that reality. Now, SHIFT is more than just a part-time job platform. It’s a full ecosystem where students, young professionals, and the diaspora come together to create solutions for real problems across Africa. From law and tech to research and entrepreneurship—we’re building multidisciplinary teams, launching local projects, and opening doors for collaboration with the world. We’re not waiting for change. We’re building it—one skill, one job, one SHIFት at a time. "

Bethlehem Solomon

Founder & CEO, SHIFት
